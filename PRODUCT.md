# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Polish civilian shooters, uniformed services (military, police) and private-sector security teams looking for high-end firearms and tactical training. They arrive from social media or word of mouth, mostly on mobile, and decide within seconds whether this team is elite or amateur. Secondary audience: international units (the company operates worldwide).

## Product Purpose

Marketing site for Perun Tac (peruntac.pl) — a Wrocław-based firearms/tactical training company. The site must sell competence: upcoming trainings, the offer, a free knowledge base (ballistics articles), the mission, and contact. Success = training sign-ups and contact requests.

## Brand Personality

Disciplined, lethal-serious, earned-not-given. Military/mercenary register with a Slavic edge — Perun is the Slavic god of thunder and war; the logo is a thunder-mark (kołowrót) with stencil lettering. Motto: "You don't get what you want, you get what you work for."

## Anti-references

- The current WebWave template site: generic white sections, default typography, no craft.
- Generic "tactical store" aesthetics: camo backgrounds, grunge Photoshop brushes, Impact font, red/black gamer vibes.
- SaaS landing-page grammar: cream backgrounds, eyebrow labels, identical card grids, hero-metric stats.

## Design Principles

1. **The photograph is the proof.** Real training photography (night ops, gas masks, K9, shoot-house) carries credibility — the design frames it cinematically, never decorates over it.
2. **Discipline in the details.** Mono coordinates, range-card labels, precise hairlines — the visual language of a range log, not a video game HUD.
3. **Earned drama.** Motion is heavy, controlled, exponential ease-outs — recoil management, not bounce.
4. **One signal color.** Storm cyan — Perun's lightning — used like a muzzle flash: rarely, and only where it matters.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 on carbon black, focus-visible states, full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative (instant reveals, no smooth-scroll hijack), semantic landmarks, Polish `lang="pl"`.
