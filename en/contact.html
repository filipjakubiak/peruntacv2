FAQ

Frequently asked questions. Didn't find your answer? Just call.

Can I book an individual course or one outside the standard offer?

Have your own idea for a course, or want to focus on a specific problem? Book an individual course with us. For experienced shooters we also offer regular one-on-one training.

I don't own a firearm. Can I take part in a course?

Of course! We keep a large and varied pool of firearms, and course participants can rent them on site. The only thing you need to do is let us know in advance.

Can I buy ammunition on site?

Yes, after arranging it beforehand with the course instructor.

Where do the courses take place?

Courses take place across the whole territory of Poland. The chosen location and directions are always included in the course description.

Where can I find information about upcoming courses?

We publish all dates in the events calendar and on our Facebook profile. If you can't find the information you're looking for, just get in touch.

Can minors take part in the courses?

Absolutely! However, a parent or legal guardian must be present.

What should I wear to the range?

Courses usually take place at outdoor ranges, so dress for the weather forecast. We work with a tactical belt, so your clothing must not block access to it. Keep in mind that clothes and footwear may get dirty, damaged or soaked.

What should I bring with me?

Courses are intensive, so we recommend bringing water and something to snack on. We also encourage you to bring a marker or a pen.

Will I receive a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, after every course participants receive personalized certificates confirming completion of the program.
