Protection isn't presence. It's an edge you never see.

Perun Security is a branch of Perun that grew out of our team's operational and training work. Protection here is handled by people with real experience: tactics instructors, former service members and operators who work under pressure every day.

We don't sell a muscular presence. We sell a plan, reconnaissance and a cool decision at the moment when every second counts. The best protection is the kind after which the client doesn't even remember something could have gone wrong.

We operate discreetly, legally and with full documentation. Every assignment starts with a risk analysis and ends with a report, because security you can't account for isn't security.

Scope of protection

Personal and VIP protection, from a single meeting to a permanent team at the client's side.

How we work

Four steps that turn anxiety into measurable, controlled risk.

Reconnaissance

A confidential conversation and a risk analysis: who you are, what exposes you, what your day, routes and surroundings look like. We don't move without it: a plan without reconnaissance is an illusion.

Plan

We select the team, the mode (discreet or overt), the procedures and the contingency variants. You get a clear scope, cost and terms of cooperation in writing.

Execution

The protection goes live: advance work, communications, constant readiness. We work so that your day looks normal while security happens in the background.

Report

After the assignment, a debrief: what happened, what to improve, what the risk looks like going forward. Security is a process, not a one-off service.
