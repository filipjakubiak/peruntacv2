PERUN TAC // TRAINING

Three modules. One standard.

A wide range of courses across many subjects and every skill level. From your first contact with a firearm to advanced specialist courses where every element has a real-world application.

MODULE / SHOOTING

Shootingcourses

Learning to run different shooting platforms and building skill systematically: from safe fundamentals to shooting on the move, at distance and under time pressure.

  • Pistol: fundamentals, trigger work, rapid fire
  • AR-15 carbine: zero, marksmanship, recoil control
  • Open training sessions with a shot timer and feedback
  • Individual training for experienced shooters

MODULE / TACTICAL

Tactical courses

Tactical operations with advanced weapon handling: close-quarters battle, small-team tactics and vehicle tactics, by day and by night.

  • CQB: from intro to kill-house scenarios
  • Small-team tactics and section-level work
  • Vehicle Tactics: working around and inside vehicles
  • Low-light / NVG operations
Upcoming dates
MODULE / SPECIALIST

Specialist courses

Specialist skills for demanding environments: close protection, operations in high-risk countries, and preparing civilian personnel for deployments to elevated-threat areas.

  • Close protection and protective operations
  • Height access courses: buildings and mountain terrain
  • Breaching: defeating structural obstacles
  • HEAT: preparation for work in risk zones

Training formats

The same standard, three ways of working. Pick the one that fits your situation.

Open OPEN ENROLLMENT

Dates from the events calendar, open enrollment and small-group work. The simplest way to start and keep raising your level. You'll find current dates in the events calendar.

Individual 1:1 TRAINING

Have your own idea for a course, or want to work on a specific problem? We build the program around you. For experienced shooters we also run regular one-on-one training.

Dedicated CLOSED GROUPS

Closed courses for organized groups: companies, formations and teams. Beyond courses, we organize tactical workshops where participants from different sectors exchange experience and pressure-test their procedures under realistic conditions.
