PERUN TAC // ABOUT US

Who we are.

Perun Tac was founded in 2020 as an answer to a real need for a reliable, substantive and practical training environment in the field of security and defense.

We don't do "playing commando".

Our mission is to deliver knowledge and skills built on experience gained in operational environments, in combat and protection teams, and in cooperation with a range of uniformed and military formations.

Instead of courses with no substance, you get professional training run by experts, where every element — from firearm handling fundamentals, through small-team tactics, to advanced specialist courses — has a real application in real-world conditions.

The company is built by people from different sectors of the security industry, with years of experience from missions in Poland and abroad. Our instructor team consists of professionals who earned their experience in military units, police, special services and the private sector.
